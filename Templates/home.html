About Mangoes

Mango is a tropical fruit that is loved by many around the world. It is a rich source of vitamins A and C, as well as dietary fiber. Mangoes come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and colors, and their taste ranges from sweet to sour. Some popular varieties of mangoes include Anwar Ratool, Chaunsa(Black), Chaunsa(Summer Bahisht), Chaunsa(White), Dosehri, Fejri, Langra, Sindhri.

Importance of Mango Classification

  • It allows farmers to identify the different varieties of mangoes that they are growing, which can help them optimize their cultivation practices.
  • It helps exporters and importers ensure that they are dealing with the correct types of mangoes, which is important for marketing and sales purposes.
  • It helps consumers choose the types of mangoes that they prefer, based on taste, texture, and other factors.
